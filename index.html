Guardian Hat
A clever invention to help the blind and the deaf.
It's simple yet smart and it will hopefully give me the 7 I need.

Why make this?

Every year, the world is becoming more urban, and every single day,
there are millions of accidents in the city. Now imagine being deaf and blind.
Tough right? This isn't easy as some people get dogs to guide them,
or a person, or a stick. But they all have their disadvantages.
Dogs are hard to find, finding a person that will stick to your whole life is hard,
and a stick isn't really practical when faced against multiple types of obstacles.
I am not saying my solution is much better than others,
I believe my solution has many flaws, but this is my approach to the problem.
